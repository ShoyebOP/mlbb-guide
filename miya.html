Miya: The Swift Sniper - MLBB Guide

Alright, here's a comprehensive MLBB guide for Miya, focusing on maximizing her potential in the current meta, including builds for Gold Lane (her primary role) and a Jungle build option.

Primary Role: Gold Lane (Marksman)

Secondary Role: Jungle (Off-Meta, but viable)

Hero Overview:

Miya is a late-game hyper-carry Marksman. Her strength lies in her ability to melt enemies with sustained damage, especially when she reaches full build. She's highly dependent on farm and positioning, making her vulnerable in the early game. She's also very reliant on allies.

Strengths:

  • High sustained damage output in late game.
  • Strong pushing and objective taking capabilities.
  • Excellent mobility and survivability with her ultimate (Turret advantage)
  • Her kit makes it very hard to pick her off.

Weaknesses:

  • Extremely weak early game.
  • Susceptible to crowd control (CC).
  • Relies heavily on items.
  • Requires good positioning to survive team fights.

I. Gold Lane (Primary Role) - Builds and Emblems

Playstyle: Farm efficiently, avoid unnecessary risks, and scale into the late game. Focus on securing last hits on minions and jungle creeps to accelerate your item acquisition. Utilize your ultimate for quick escapes or aggressive plays when necessary.

A. Build 1: Standard Crit/Attack Speed Build

  • Item Build:
    1. Swift Boots: (Early game movement speed and attack speed)
    2. Berserker's Fury: (Critical chance and damage)
    3. Scarlet Phantom: (Attack Speed, Crit Chance, Movement Speed buff on crit)
    4. Windtalker: (Attack Speed, Crit Chance, Movement Speed; Passive helps with wave clear and AoE damage)
    5. Blade of Despair: (High Physical Attack, Passive that increases damage against enemies with low HP)
    6. Endless Battle: (Hybrid Stats - Physical Attack, Mana Regen, HP, Cooldown Reduction, Movement Speed, Physical Lifesteal. Passive True Damage every 3 seconds after using a skill).
  • Emblem: Marksman Emblem
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed)
    • Tier 2: Weapon Master (Increases Physical Attack from Equipment)
    • Tier 3: Weakness Finder (Basic attacks have a chance to reduce the enemy's movement speed and attack speed)

B. Build 2: High Attack Speed/Lifesteal

  • Item Build:
    1. Swift Boots:
    2. Scarlet Phantom:
    3. Windtalker:
    4. Demon Hunter Sword: (Attack Speed, Physical Lifesteal, Passive that deals % current HP damage)
    5. Golden Staff: (Attack Speed, Passive that converts crit chance into attack speed, synergizes well with Demon Hunter Sword)
    6. Immortality: (Survival in late game)
  • Emblem: Marksman Emblem
    • Tier 1: Agility
    • Tier 2: Weapon Master
    • Tier 3: Electro Flash (Using a Basic Attack inflicts damage and restores HP. Grants additional Movement Speed)

Counter Items (Gold Lane):

  • Against Burst Damage (e.g., Eudora, Aurora, Kadita):
    • Replace Endless Battle/Blade of Despair with Wind of Nature: (Provides Physical Damage Immunity for a short duration - use it proactively when you anticipate being targeted).
  • Against High Crowd Control (e.g., Khufra, Atlas, Vale):
    • Replace Boots with Tough Boots: (Reduces Crowd Control duration).
    • Consider Purify as your battle spell instead of Flicker.
  • Against High Physical Defense (e.g., Akai, Hylos):
    • Malefic Roar Replace a Crit item (Scarlet Phantom/Windtalker) to penetrate armor.

II. Jungle (Off-Meta) - Builds and Emblems

Playstyle: Focus on farming efficiently in the early game to gain a level and gold advantage. Prioritize ganking lanes with good setup potential or vulnerable enemies. Rotate to objectives like Turtle and Lord to secure advantages for your team. Be mindful of your positioning in team fights, as you're still a squishy target.

A. Build 1: Standard Crit Jungle

  • Item Build:
    1. Hunter's Knife > Raptor Machete: (Jungle item - enhances jungle clearing and provides retribution)
    2. Swift Boots:
    3. Berserker's Fury:
    4. Scarlet Phantom:
    5. Windtalker:
    6. Blade of Despair:
  • Emblem: Assassin Emblem
    • Tier 1: Agility
    • Tier 2: Weapon Master
    • Tier 3: Killing Spree (Killing a hero restores HP and increases Movement Speed)

B. Build 2: Attack Speed/Sustain Jungle

  • Item Build:
    1. Hunter's Knife > Raptor Machete:
    2. Swift Boots:
    3. Demon Hunter Sword:
    4. Golden Staff:
    5. Scarlet Phantom:
    6. Immortality:
  • Emblem: Assassin Emblem
    • Tier 1: Agility
    • Tier 2: Weapon Master
    • Tier 3: Killing Spree

Counter Items (Jungle):

  • Against High Burst Damage (e.g., Gusion, Lancelot):
    • Consider Rose Gold Meteor as a replacement for a damage item (Provides a shield when HP is low).
  • Against Tanky Junglers (e.g., Barats, Fredrinn):
    • Prioritize Demon Hunter Sword in your build.
    • Focus on kiting and dealing sustained damage.
  • Against Crowd Control heavy teams:
    • Tough Boots is crucial.

General Tips for Miya:

  • Positioning is Key: Always stay behind your tanks and fighters in team fights. Avoid being the primary target.
  • Abuse your Ultimate: Use it to reposition, dodge skills, or escape from dangerous situations. Don't be afraid to use it defensively.
  • Communicate with your team: Let them know when you're ready to engage or need assistance.
  • Farm efficiently: Prioritize farming minions and jungle creeps to accelerate your item progression.
  • Practice makes perfect: Miya requires good mechanical skill and map awareness to play effectively.

Skill Usage:

  • Passive (Turbo): Stacking this before a fight is great for maximum damage and attack speed.
  • First Skill (Fission Shot): Use this to push lanes quickly or to deal extra damage to enemies in team fights.
  • Second Skill (Rain of Arrows): This skill is great for zoning enemies or slowing them down. Use it to set up kills for yourself or your teammates.
  • Ultimate (Turbo Stealth): This skill is your most important skill. Use it to reposition, dodge enemy skills, or to escape from dangerous situations.

Combo:

  • The optimal combo for Miya is to start with her second skill (Rain of Arrows) to slow down the enemy, followed by her first skill (Fission Shot) to deal extra damage. Once the enemy is slowed down, use your ultimate (Turbo Stealth) to get into a better position and deal even more damage.
