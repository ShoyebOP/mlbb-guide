Hanzo: The Akuma Ninja - A Comprehensive Guide

Alright, let's craft a comprehensive Mobile Legends: Bang Bang guide for Hanzo!

Primary Role: Assassin

Lane: Jungle

Hanzo's Strengths:

  • High Burst Damage
  • Excellent Mobility (with Demon Pneuma)
  • Can Farm Safely from a Distance
  • Good at Securing Objectives

Hanzo's Weaknesses:

  • Very Squishy in his Original Form
  • Highly Dependent on Demon Pneuma
  • Vulnerable to Crowd Control
  • Can be Easily Countered by Good Map Awareness

Gameplay

Hanzo is a unique assassin that relies on his Demon Pneuma to deal damage and secure objectives. He is best played in the jungle where he can farm safely and gank lanes effectively.

Early Game:

Focus on farming jungle camps and stacking Demon Blood. Use your Demon Pneuma to scout ahead and secure objectives like the Lithowanderer.

Mid Game:

Look for opportunities to gank lanes with your Demon Pneuma. Be mindful of your positioning and don't overextend. Secure objectives like Turtle and Lord.

Late Game:

Use your Demon Pneuma to burst down high-priority targets in team fights. Be mindful of your original body's location and don't get caught out of position.
