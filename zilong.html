Zilong: The Son of the Dragon - A Comprehensive Guide

Alright, here's a comprehensive MLBB guide for Zilong, focusing on his primary and secondary lane roles, builds, emblems, talents, counter items, and considerations for ease of use.

Primary Lane: Gold Lane (Roamer Secondary)

Overview:

Zilong is a strong damage-dealing fighter, specializing in burst damage, split-pushing, and chasing down enemies. His passive, "Dragon Flurry," enhances his next basic attack after using a skill, allowing him to rapidly take down opponents.

Gold Lane Zilong

Playstyle: Zilong Gold Lane focuses on farming early, harassing the enemy marksman, and scaling into a late-game threat. Your goal is to snowball your gold advantage and push towers quickly.

Emblem: Assassin or Fighter

  • Assassin Emblem:
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed +) - Helps with rotation
    • Tier 2: Invasion (Physical Penetration +) - Enhance damage to enemies with high defence
    • Tier 3: Killing Spree (Killing an enemy grants HP regen and Movement Speed) - For snowballing.
  • Fighter Emblem:
    • Tier 1: Bravery (Physical Attack +) - More damage
    • Tier 2: Swift (Attack Speed +) - More basic attacks
    • Tier 3: Festival of Blood (Spell Vamp +) - Sustain in lane and team fights.

Builds:

Build 1: Standard High Damage
  • Item Build:
    1. / Warrior Boots / Tough Boots: (Adapt to enemy team composition)
    2. Berserker's Fury: (Critical Chance & Damage)
    3. Windtalker: (Attack Speed, Critical Chance & Movement Speed)
    4. Scarlet Phantom: (Attack Speed, Critical Chance & Movement Speed)
    5. Blade of Despair: (High Physical Attack)
    6. Immortality/Malefic Roar: (Late game survivability or further damage)
Build 2: Split Push & Sustained Damage
  • Item Build:
    1. / Warrior Boots / Tough Boots: (Adapt to enemy team composition)
    2. Berserker's Fury: (Critical Chance & Damage)
    3. Windtalker: (Attack Speed, Critical Chance & Movement Speed)
    4. Scarlet Phantom: (Attack Speed, Critical Chance & Movement Speed)
    5. Endless Battle: (True Damage & Stats)
    6. Immortality/Malefic Roar: (Late game survivability or further damage)

Counter Items and Situational Swaps:

  • Against High Burst Mages (e.g., Eudora, Vale):
    • Swap Immortality for Athena's Shield or Radiant Armor. Athena's Shield provides magic damage reduction against initial burst, while Radiant Armor provides sustained magic damage reduction.
  • Against Heavy Crowd Control (e.g., Aurora, Khufra):
    • Consider Tough Boots early. You can also swap Windtalker for Rose Gold Meteor in the late game for a magic damage shield and increased physical attack when your HP is low. This is a more aggressive choice.
  • Against High HP Tanks (e.g., Hylos, Barats):
    • Keep Malefic Roar in your build to shred armor.
  • Against Lifesteal heroes (e.g. Alucard, Thamuz):
    • Dominance Ice - Reduce Movement Speed, Attack Speed and regen.
  • Against Revival items (e.g. Immortality, Winter Truncheon):
    • Sea Halberd - Reduce regeneration and shield effect

Gameplay Tips (Gold Lane):

  • Early Game (Levels 1-4): Focus on last-hitting minions for gold. Harass the enemy marksman when possible, but prioritize farming. Be wary of ganks.
  • Mid Game (Levels 5-12): Look for opportunities to rotate to other lanes and assist with ganks. Start pushing towers when you have a gold advantage.
  • Late Game (Levels 13-15): Split-push effectively. Use your mobility to quickly take down towers and pressure the enemy base. Be mindful of team fights and join them when necessary.
  • Ultimate Usage: Use your ultimate ("Dragon Flurry") to chase down fleeing enemies or to quickly burst down a single target. The increased attack speed and movement speed are crucial for securing kills.
  • Skill Combo: A common combo is Skill 2 (Spear Flip) > Skill 1 (Spear Strike) > Basic Attack (Enhanced by Passive) > Ultimate (Dragon Flurry) to chase and finish.

Roamer Zilong (Secondary)

Playstyle: As a Roamer, Zilong focuses on roaming, ganking, and setting up kills for his team. This role requires good map awareness and communication.

Emblem: Assassin

  • Talent 1: Agility (Movement Speed +)
  • Talent 2: Invasion (Physical Penetration +)
  • Talent 3: Killing Spree (Killing an enemy grants HP regen and Movement Speed) - For snowballing.

Builds:

Build 1: Utility Roamer
  • Courage Mask: (Mandatory Roam Item)
  • / Warrior Boots / Tough Boots: (Adapt to enemy team composition)
  • Blade of the Heptaseas: (Damage and burst potential after hiding in bush)
  • Hunter Strike: (CDR and Movement Speed after hitting enemy)
  • Immortality: (Survivability)
  • Malefic Roar: (Armor Penetration - late game)
Build 2: Damage Roamer
  • Courage Mask: (Mandatory Roam Item)
  • / Warrior Boots / Tough Boots: (Adapt to enemy team composition)
  • Berserker's Fury: (Critical Chance & Damage)
  • Blade of Despair: (High Physical Attack)
  • Immortality: (Survivability)
  • Hunter Strike: (CDR and Movement Speed after hitting enemy)

Counter Items and Situational Swaps:

  • Against High HP Tanks (e.g., Hylos, Barats):
    • Keep Malefic Roar in your build to shred armor.
  • Against Lifesteal heroes (e.g. Alucard, Thamuz):
    • Dominance Ice - Reduce Movement Speed, Attack Speed and regen.
  • Against Revival items (e.g. Immortality, Winter Truncheon):
    • Sea Halberd - Reduce regeneration and shield effect

Gameplay Tips (Roamer):

  • Early Game: Secure vision in key areas. Help your jungler secure buffs. Gank lanes that are vulnerable.
  • Mid Game: Rotate constantly to different lanes to apply pressure. Prioritize ganking the mid lane to give your mage an advantage. Focus on objectives (towers, turtle, lord).
  • Late Game: Peel for your carries in team fights. Use your mobility to initiate fights or to pick off isolated enemies.
  • Communication: Coordinate ganks and objective pushes with your team. Use the in-game chat to communicate your intentions.
  • Bush Camping: Zilong's burst potential is amplified by ambushing enemies from bushes.

Teamfight Engagement:

As a Zilong roamer, your job is to dive on the enemy backline or punish out-of-position heroes.

General Zilong Tips:

  • Practice Skill Combos: Zilong's effectiveness relies on executing his skill combos smoothly.
  • Map Awareness: Be constantly aware of the minimap to track enemy positions and identify ganking opportunities.
  • Adapt Your Build: Don't be afraid to adjust your build based on the enemy team composition.
  • Positioning: Position yourself carefully in team fights to avoid getting focused down.
  • Learn Enemy Skill Cool-downs: Understanding when enemy heroes have used their important crowd control skills can allow you to engage more aggressively.

This guide provides a solid foundation for playing Zilong effectively in Mobile Legends. Remember to practice and adapt your playstyle to different situations and enemy team compositions. Good luck!
