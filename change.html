Chang'e: The Moon Palace Immortal - A Comprehensive Guide

Okay, here's a comprehensive MLBB guide for Chang'e, covering her ideal and secondary lane, multiple builds, emblems, talents, and counter itemization:

Overview:

Chang'e is a powerful burst mage specializing in long-range poke and sustained damage. She excels at clearing waves quickly, harassing enemies, and providing vision. Her ultimate, Meteor Shower, is a game-changer in team fights. She’s best played in Mid-lane due to her requirement of early game farming, but can also be played as Gold-lane when needed.

Primary Lane: Mid-Lane

Chang'e's natural habitat. She needs the farm and experience from the mid-lane to reach her full potential. She can quickly push lanes and roam to help other lanes.

Builds (Mid-Lane):

Build 1: Classic Burst (Easy to Use)

Purpose: High burst damage for quickly eliminating squishy targets and applying pressure.

  • Item Build:
    1. Magic Shoes: Movement Speed + Mana Regen.
    2. Enchanted Talisman: Mana Regen, Cooldown Reduction, Magic Power. Essential for spamming skills early game.
    3. Lightning Truncheon: Magic Power, Mana, Cooldown Reduction. Amplifies damage based on mana, synergizes well with Chang'e's playstyle.
    4. Holy Crystal: Significant Magic Power increase.
    5. Divine Glaive: Magic Penetration based on enemy magic defense.
    6. Blood Wings: Magic Power, HP. Provides survivability and further boosts magic power.
  • Emblem: Mage
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed) or Mastery (Cooldown Reduction) – Personal Preference.
    • Tier 2: Observation (Magic Penetration).
    • Tier 3: Magic Worship (Deals damage equal to 8% of the target's max HP 3 times within 5 seconds, the target will take 1.2% of the target's max HP as extra damage).

Explanation: This build focuses on maximizing Chang'e's burst potential. Enchanted Talisman and Lightning Truncheon provides early game survivability in terms of mana-regen. Magic Penetration from Divine Glaive ensures she can deal significant damage even to magic resistant targets.

Build 2: Sustained Damage/Poke (More Aggressive)

Purpose: For sustained damage output, excellent for prolonged engagements and dealing with tankier enemies.

  • Item Build:
    1. Magic Shoes: Movement Speed + Mana Regen.
    2. Enchanted Talisman: Mana Regen, Cooldown Reduction, Magic Power.
    3. Glowing Wand: Magic Power, Movement Speed, HP. Burn effect that deals damage over time.
    4. Ice Queen Wand: Magic Power, Movement Speed, Lifesteal, Slow. Provides sustain and crowd control.
    5. Holy Crystal: Significant Magic Power increase.
    6. Divine Glaive: Magic Penetration based on enemy magic defense.
  • Emblem: Mage
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed) or Mastery (Cooldown Reduction) – Personal Preference.
    • Tier 2: Observation (Magic Penetration).
    • Tier 3: Magic Worship (Deals damage equal to 8% of the target's max HP 3 times within 5 seconds, the target will take 1.2% of the target's max HP as extra damage).

Explanation: Glowing Wand and Ice Queen Wand provides extra effects that synergizes well for early to mid game advantages.

Secondary Lane: Gold Lane (Less Ideal, but Viable)

Chang'e can be played in the Gold Lane, especially if your team needs a ranged damage dealer who can scale well into the late game. She's vulnerable early, so focus on farming safely.

Builds (Gold Lane):

Build 1: Safe Gold Laner (Focus on Farm)

Purpose: Prioritize safe farming and scaling. Good against aggressive laners where you need to play defensively.

  • Item Build:
    1. Arcane Boots: Magic Penetration + Movement Speed.
    2. Clock of Destiny: HP, Mana, Magic Power. Provides early game bulk and scaling.
    3. Lightning Truncheon: Magic Power, Mana, Cooldown Reduction.
    4. Holy Crystal: Significant Magic Power increase.
    5. Genius Wand: Magic Power, Movement Speed, Magic Defense Reduction.
    6. Immortality: Defense, HP, Resurrect. Offers a safety net for late-game team fights.
  • Emblem: Mage
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed).
    • Tier 2: Observation (Magic Penetration).
    • Tier 3: Impure Rage (Deals damage with skills, gaining extra damage and mana regen).

Explanation: Clock of Destiny gives early game bulk to survive early game clashes. Immortality is crucial for surviving ganks and late-game teamfights. Impure Rage helps sustain mana when farming.

Build 2: Aggressive Gold Laner (For Winning Lane)

Purpose: To aggressively win the lane and push for an early advantage.

  • Item Build:
    1. Arcane Boots: Magic Penetration + Movement Speed.
    2. Enchanted Talisman: Mana Regen, Cooldown Reduction, Magic Power.
    3. Glowing Wand: Magic Power, Movement Speed, HP.
    4. Holy Crystal: Significant Magic Power increase.
    5. Divine Glaive: Magic Penetration based on enemy magic defense.
    6. Blood Wings: Magic Power, HP.
  • Emblem: Mage
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed).
    • Tier 2: Observation (Magic Penetration).
    • Tier 3: Magic Worship (Deals damage equal to 8% of the target's max HP 3 times within 5 seconds, the target will take 1.2% of the target's max HP as extra damage).

Explanation: Glowing Wand gives sustained damage to pressure enemies in lane. Blood Wings gives late-game damage and survivability.

Counter Itemization:

Here's how to adapt your build depending on the enemy team composition:

  • Against High Magic Resistance (e.g., Esmeralda, Uranus, Oracle):
    • Swap: Replace Blood Wings (or a less essential item) with Genius Wand (Magic Power, Movement Speed, Magic Defense Reduction) or a second Divine Glaive if they are stacking excessive magic resistance.
  • Against High Burst Damage (e.g., Saber, Eudora, Gusion):
    • Swap: Replace Blood Wings or Divine Glaive with Winter Truncheon (Magic Power, Armor, HP, Active: Freeze yourself for 2 seconds). Use the active to avoid a fatal burst. Alternatively, Athena's Shield (HP, Magic Resist, CD reduction, Shield) is a strong defensive option.
  • Against Heavy Crowd Control (e.g., Chou, Atlas, Khufra):
    • Prioritize positioning and team communication. Consider using Purify as a battle spell instead of Flicker. A defensive item like Tough Boots (+Magic Defense, reduces CC duration) early game can help.
  • Against High Lifesteal/Spell Vamp (e.g., Yu Zhong, Alucard):
    • Consider: While Chang'e's damage is generally high enough to burst through sustain, having a teammate build Sea Halberd (Physical heroes) or Necklace of Durance (Magical heroes) will significantly reduce healing.

General Chang'e Tips:

  • Positioning is Key: Stay behind your tanks and supports. Chang'e is vulnerable to assassins and divers.
  • Use Enhanced Basic Attack Wisely: After using a skill, Chang'e's next basic attack deals extra magic damage. Use this to harass enemies in lane.
  • Ultimate Timing: Don't just blindly use Meteor Shower. Use it when enemies are grouped up or when you have a clear shot at a priority target. Consider the enemy's CC abilities before ulting.
  • Roam Effectively: After clearing your lane, roam to help other lanes or secure objectives.
  • Vision is Important: Use your first skill, "Starmoon Shock," to scout bushes and check for enemy ambushes.
  • Practice makes perfect: Learn to aim your skills effectively.
  • Adapt your build: Don't be afraid to adjust your build based on the enemy team composition.
