Badang: The Fist of Freedom - A Comprehensive Guide

Okay, here's a comprehensive MLBB guide for Badang, focusing on his primary and secondary lanes, builds, emblems, talents, and counter-items. I've prioritized effective and relatively easy-to-use builds.

Hero Role: Fighter

Primary Lane: EXP Lane

Secondary Lane: Roaming

Badang EXP Lane Guide

Role Overview: Badang in the EXP lane focuses on harassing the enemy laner, securing farm, and rotating to help team fights. His high burst damage and crowd control make him a threat in skirmishes.

Playstyle:

  • Early Game: Focus on last-hitting minions and poking the enemy with basic attacks and Qigong Fist. Avoid overextending without vision.
  • Mid Game: Look for opportunities to rotate to mid-lane or the jungle to assist in ganks. Use your ultimate, Fist Break, to secure kills.
  • Late Game: Stick with your team and initiate fights with Fist Break. Focus on locking down priority targets like the enemy Marksman or Mage.

Build 1: High Damage Burst

  • Item Build:
    1. / Warrior Boots/Tough Boots: (Choose based on enemy team composition: Warrior Boots for physical damage, Tough Boots for magic damage and crowd control reduction)
    2. Bloodlust Axe: (Sustain, Spell Vamp)
    3. Hunter Strike: (Cooldown Reduction, Physical Attack, Movement Speed)
    4. Blade of Despair: (High Physical Attack, passive increases damage to enemies below 50% HP)
    5. Malefic Roar: (Physical Penetration, useful against tanks)
    6. Immortality: (Revive, Survivability)
  • Emblem: Assassin
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed)
    • Tier 2: Invasion (Physical Penetration)
    • Tier 3: Killing Spree (Restores HP and increases Movement Speed after a kill)

Explanation: This build aims to maximize Badang's damage output. Bloodlust Axe provides sustain, Hunter Strike helps with mobility and CDR, and Blade of Despair and Malefic Roar deal massive damage. Immortality allows for a second chance in fights.

Build 2: Durable Fighter

  • Item Build:
    1. / Warrior Boots/Tough Boots: (Choose based on enemy team composition: Warrior Boots for physical damage, Tough Boots for magic damage and crowd control reduction)
    2. Bloodlust Axe: (Sustain, Spell Vamp)
    3. War Axe: (Adaptive Attack, HP, CDR, Movement Speed)
    4. Endless Battle: (Hybrid Stats: Physical Attack, Mana Regen, HP, CDR, Movement Speed, True Damage on basic attack after skill use)
    5. Immortality: (Revive, Survivability)
    6. Brute Force Breastplate: (HP, Physical Defense, Increases movement speed and defense with each skill/basic attack cast.)
  • Emblem: Fighter
    • Tier 1: Bravery (Physical Attack)
    • Tier 2: Invasion (Physical Penetration)
    • Tier 3: Disabling Strike (Increases damage to enemies affected by crowd control.)

Explanation: This build focuses on making Badang more durable and able to survive longer in team fights. War Axe and Brute Force Breastplate provide increased tankiness while still dealing considerable damage with Endless Battle.

Counter Items (EXP Lane):

  • Against High Magic Damage:
    • Athena's Shield: (Magic Defense, HP, Shield) - Replace Malefic Roar or Blade of Despair.
  • Against High Physical Damage:
    • Dominance Ice: (Mana, Physical Defense, Cooldown Reduction, Reduces enemy healing and attack speed) - Replace Blade of Despair or Malefic Roar.
    • Blade Armor: (Physical Defense, reflects damage back to the attacker) - Replace Blade of Despair or Malefic Roar.
  • Against High Regen/Lifesteal:
    • Sea Halberd: (Physical Attack, Attack Speed, Reduces enemy healing) - Replace Malefic Roar or Blade of Despair.
    • Dominance Ice: (Mana, Physical Defense, Cooldown Reduction, Reduces enemy healing and attack speed) - Replace Blade of Despair or Malefic Roar.
  • Against heroes that can kite easily(Claude, Wanwan, Irithel etc):
    • Wind of Nature: (Physical Attack, Attack Speed, Physical Lifesteal, makes hero immune to all physical damage for a short period of time) - Replace Malefic Roar or Blade of Despair.

Badang Roaming Guide

Role Overview: As a Roamer, Badang excels at setting up ganks, peeling for his teammates, and initiating fights. His strong crowd control and burst damage can turn the tide of a battle.

Playstyle:

  • Early Game: Prioritize helping your Jungler secure objectives like the Lithowanderer and Blue Buff. Roam to other lanes to set up ganks.
  • Mid Game: Focus on controlling vision and rotating to help your teammates. Use your ultimate, Fist Break, to initiate fights and lock down key enemy targets.
  • Late Game: Protect your backline and look for opportunities to disrupt the enemy team with Fist Break.

Build 1: Tank Roam

  • Item Build:
    1. Courage Mask: (Increases team's physical and magical attack)
    2. Tough Boots: (Magic Defense, Crowd Control Reduction)
    3. Dominance Ice: (Mana, Physical Defense, Cooldown Reduction, Reduces enemy healing and attack speed)
    4. Guardian Helmet: (HP Regen, HP)
    5. Athena's Shield: (Magic Defense, HP, Shield)
    6. Immortality: (Revive, Survivability)
  • Emblem: Tank
    • Tier 1: Vitality (HP)
    • Tier 2: Tenacity (Defense when Crowd Controlled)
    • Tier 3: Brave Smite (Restores HP when dealing crowd control)

Explanation: This build focuses on making Badang a durable and impactful roamer. Courage Mask supports the team. Tough Boots provide Crowd Control reduction. Dominance Ice provides utility by reducing enemy healing and attack speed. Guardian Helmet and Athena's Shield provide sustain and magic resistance. Immortality gives a second chance in fights.

Build 2: Damage Roam

  • Item Build:
    1. Courage Mask: (Increases team's physical and magical attack)
    2. Tough Boots: (Magic Defense, Crowd Control Reduction)
    3. Bloodlust Axe: (Spell Vamp, Damage)
    4. War Axe: (Adaptive Attack, HP, CDR, Movement Speed)
    5. Brute Force Breastplate: (HP, Physical Defense, Increases movement speed and defense with each skill/basic attack cast.)
    6. Immortality: (Revive, Survivability)
  • Emblem: Assassin
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed)
    • Tier 2: Invasion (Physical Penetration)
    • Tier 3: Killing Spree (Restores HP and increases Movement Speed after a kill)

Explanation: This build allows Badang to deal significant damage while roaming. The combination of Bloodlust Axe, War Axe, and Brute Force Breastplate provides sustain, damage, and survivability.

Counter Items (Roaming):

  • Against High Magic Damage:
    • Athena's Shield: (Magic Defense, HP, Shield) - Replace Brute Force Breastplate or War Axe.
  • Against High Physical Damage:
    • Blade Armor: (Physical Defense, reflects damage back to the attacker) - Replace Brute Force Breastplate or War Axe.
  • Against Crowd Control Heavy Teams:
    • Tough Boots: Buy this instead of Warrior Boots.
  • Against enemies that can kite easily(Claude, Wanwan, Irithel etc):
    • Wind of Nature: (Physical Attack, Attack Speed, Physical Lifesteal, makes hero immune to all physical damage for a short period of time) - Replace Brute Force Breastplate or War Axe.
