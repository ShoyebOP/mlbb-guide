Vexana: The Twisted Summoner - A Comprehensive Guide

Alright, here's a comprehensive MLBB guide for Vexana, covering her primary and secondary roles, builds, emblems, talents, counter items, and hero matchups.

Primary Role: Mage

Best Lane: Mid Lane

Secondary Role: Roamer (Support)

Vexana Overview:

Vexana is a mage hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang known for her:

  • Area-of-Effect (AoE) Damage: Her skills deal damage to multiple enemies, making her effective in team fights and wave clearing.
  • Crowd Control (CC): Her skills can terrify, knock up, and pull enemies, disrupting their positioning and making them vulnerable.
  • Ultimate: Summons a powerful Undead Knight that fights alongside her, providing additional damage and tankiness.
  • Good Wave Clear: Can quickly clear minion waves with her skills.

Vexana's Weaknesses:

  • Low Mobility: Vexana is very immobile, making her vulnerable to ganks and assassins.
  • Skill-Shot Dependent: Her effectiveness relies on landing her skill shots, particularly her first skill.
  • Squishy: She has low HP and defenses, making her an easy target for burst damage.
  • Mana Hungry: She can run out of mana quickly, especially in the early game.

I. Mid Lane Vexana

Playstyle: As a Mid Laner, Vexana focuses on controlling the lane, harassing the enemy mid-laner, and rotating to help other lanes. She excels at pushing waves and dealing damage from a safe distance.

Build 1: Burst Damage

  • Emblem: Mage
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed)
    • Tier 2: Observation (Magic Penetration)
    • Tier 3: Magic Worship (Burn Effect)
  • Items:
    1. Arcane Boots: Magic Penetration for increased damage.
    2. Clock of Destiny: HP, Mana, and Magic Power that scales over time.
    3. Lightning Truncheon: Magic Power, Mana, and Cooldown Reduction. Deals extra magic damage based on mana.
    4. Holy Crystal: High Magic Power boost.
    5. Divine Glaive: Magic Penetration to counter magic resistance.
    6. Blood Wings: Magic Power and HP for increased survivability.

Explanation: This build maximizes Vexana's burst damage potential. Clock of Destiny provides scaling stats. Lightning Truncheon synergizes with her mana pool. Holy Crystal and Blood Wings provide significant magic power boosts. Divine Glaive helps penetrate magic resistance.

Build 2: Sustain and Cooldown Reduction

  • Emblem: Mage
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed)
    • Tier 2: Observation (Magic Penetration)
    • Tier 3: Impure Rage (Mana Regen and Extra Damage)
  • Items:
    1. Demon Shoes: Mana Regeneration.
    2. Clock of Destiny: HP, Mana, and Magic Power.
    3. Enchanted Talisman: Magic Power, HP, Cooldown Reduction, and Mana Regeneration.
    4. Holy Crystal: High Magic Power boost.
    5. Glowing Wand: Magic Power, Movement Speed, and a burning effect that deals damage over time.
    6. Immortality: Provides a second life.

Explanation: This build focuses on sustain and cooldown reduction, allowing Vexana to cast her skills more frequently. Enchanted Talisman provides mana regeneration and cooldown reduction. Glowing Wand adds sustained damage over time. Immortality provides a safety net.

Counter Items (Mid Lane):

  • Against High Burst Mages (e.g., Eudora, Aurora):
    • Replace Blood Wings/Holy Crystal with Athena's Shield: Provides a magic damage shield.
  • Against High Magic Resistance (e.g., Esmeralda, Uranus):
    • Replace Blood Wings/Immortality with Genius Wand: Reduces enemy magic defense.
  • Against Assassins (e.g., Ling, Gusion):
    • Replace Blood Wings/Holy Crystal with Winter Truncheon: Active skill to become invulnerable for a short duration.

II. Roamer Vexana (Support)

Role Focus: Crowd Control, Vision, Team Fight Disruption

Build Philosophy: Roamer Vexana prioritizes utility, cooldown reduction, and survivability. She aims to set up kills for her team and control the flow of team fights.

Build 1: Utility/Support

  • Emblem: Support
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed)
    • Tier 2: Recovery (Hybrid Regen)
    • Tier 3: Focusing Mark (Increase damage dealt to enemies after hitting them with skills)
  • Items:
    1. Courage Mask (Roam): Provides movement speed and buffs to nearby allies.
    2. Magic Shoes: Cooldown Reduction.
    3. Enchanted Talisman: Mana Regen, Cooldown Reduction, and Magic Power.
    4. Necklace of Durance: Reduces enemy HP regen.
    5. Immortality: Provides a second life.
    6. Athena's Shield: Magic damage reduction.

Explanation: This build focuses on providing utility and support for your team. Courage Mask enhances your team's movement speed and damage. Enchanted Talisman provides mana regeneration and cooldown reduction. Necklace of Durance counters healing heroes. Immortality and Athena's Shield provide survivability.

Build 2: Tanky Support

  • Emblem: Tank
    • Tier 1: Vitality (HP)
    • Tier 2: Inspire (Cooldown Reduction)
    • Tier 3: Brave Smite (HP Regen on CC)
  • Items:
    1. Courage Mask (Roam): Provides movement speed and buffs to nearby allies.
    2. Tough Boots: Magic Resistance and reduced CC duration.
    3. Dominance Ice: Reduces enemy movement speed and attack speed, provides mana, and helps with anti-heal.
    4. Antique Cuirass: Reduces physical attack damage from enemies.
    5. Athena's Shield: Provides a magic damage shield.
    6. Immortality: Provides a second life.

Explanation: This build focuses on maximizing Vexana's tankiness and survivability. It allows her to soak up damage and disrupt the enemy team more effectively.

Counter Items (Roamer):

  • Against High Physical Damage:
    • Replace Athena's Shield with Blade Armor.
  • Against Heavy Crowd Control:
    • Prioritize Tough Boots.

General Vexana Tips & Tricks:

  • Skill Priority: Max Skill 1 (Charmed Specter) first for wave clear and poke. Then, max Skill 2 (Nether Snare). Level up your ultimate whenever possible.
  • Positioning: Stay behind your tanks and fighters in team fights. Vexana is very squishy and vulnerable to assassins.
  • Skill Usage:
    • Skill 1 (Charmed Specter): Use this to poke enemies, clear minion waves, and check bushes.
    • Skill 2 (Nether Snare): Use this to zone enemies, set up kills, or peel for your teammates.
    • Ultimate (Cursed Oath): Use this to summon an Undead Knight to fight alongside you. The Undead Knight deals significant damage and can tank damage for you.
  • Combos:
    • Basic Combo: Skill 1 -> Skill 2 -> Basic Attacks (for poke and harass)
    • Full Combo: Skill 1 -> Skill 2 -> Ultimate -> Skill 1 -> Skill 2 (for maximum damage and CC)
  • Map Awareness: Pay attention to the minimap and be aware of enemy movements. Rotate to help other lanes when needed.
  • Team Communication: Communicate with your team about your intentions and cooldowns.

Hero Matchups:

  • Strong Against: Heroes with low mobility, heroes who rely on channeling abilities (e.g., Pharsa, Odette), heroes who clump together.
  • Weak Against: High mobility assassins (e.g., Ling, Fanny, Hayabusa), heroes with strong burst damage (e.g., Eudora, Vale), heroes with long-range poke (e.g., Yve, Pharsa).

This guide provides a solid foundation for playing Vexana in Mobile Legends. Remember to adapt your build and playstyle based on the specific game situation and enemy team composition. Good luck, and have fun dominating the Land of Dawn with Vexana!
