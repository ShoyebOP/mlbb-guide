Terizla: The Executioner - A Comprehensive Guide

Alright, here's a comprehensive MLBB guide for Terizla, covering his primary and secondary lanes, effective builds, emblems, talents, counter items, and tips for easy usability.

Primary Role: EXP Lane

Secondary Role: Roaming (more of a niche pick, but can work in specific team compositions)

Overview:

Terizla is a formidable fighter hero known for his durability, crowd control, and high burst damage in late-game team fights. He excels at disrupting the enemy backline and absorbing damage, making him a valuable asset in any team. While he lacks mobility, his slow effects and area damage make him difficult to ignore.

I. EXP Lane Terizla

Role: Durable Damage Dealer, Lane Bully, Team Fight Disruptor

Playstyle: In the EXP lane, Terizla aims to establish lane dominance early by trading blows effectively with his passive and skills. Focus on farming efficiently to get ahead in levels and items. In team fights, position yourself to disrupt the enemy backline, using your ultimate to lock down key targets and inflict massive damage.

Build 1: Bruiser DPS (Balanced)

  • Item Build:
    1. / Warrior Boots/Tough Boots: Choose Warrior Boots for physical defense against heavy physical damage dealers or Tough Boots for magic defense and reduced crowd control duration.
    2. Bloodlust Axe: Core item for sustain and spell vamp, allowing you to stay in lane longer and heal during fights.
    3. War Axe: Provides HP, physical attack, and cooldown reduction, enhancing Terizla's damage output and survivability. Its passive grants additional damage and movement speed, making Terizla even stronger in prolonged engagements.
    4. Hunter Strike: Provides physical attack, physical penetration, and cooldown reduction. Its passive grants additional movement speed after dealing damage to an enemy multiple times, allowing Terizla to stick to targets more easily.
    5. Immortality: A crucial defensive item that offers a second life, allowing Terizla to continue fighting even after being taken down.
    6. Brute Force Breastplate: Another defensive item that provides physical defense, HP, and cooldown reduction. Its passive grants additional movement speed and physical/magic defense, further enhancing Terizla's survivability and mobility.
  • Emblem: Fighter Emblem
    • Tier 1: Bravery (Physical Attack)
    • Tier 2: Invasion (Physical Penetration)
    • Tier 3: Festival of Blood (Spell Vamp)

Explanation: This build focuses on providing a balance between damage and survivability, making Terizla a formidable force in both 1v1 situations and team fights.

Build 2: Tanky Sustain

  • Item Build:
    1. / Warrior Boots/Tough Boots: (Same as above)
    2. Bloodlust Axe: (Same as above)
    3. War Axe: (Same as above)
    4. Radiant Armor: Magic damage reduction and regen.
    5. Antique Cuirass: Physical damage reduction, especially against burst.
    6. Immortality: (Same as above)
  • Emblem: Tank Emblem
    • Tier 1: Vitality (HP)
    • Tier 2: Firmness (Physical Defense)
    • Tier 3: Brave Smite (HP regen on crowd control)

Explanation: This build maximizes survivability and sustain, allowing Terizla to absorb significant damage and stay in the fight for longer. The Tank emblem is ideal for a more supportive and disruptive playstyle.

Counter Items (EXP Lane):

  • Against High Regen/Lifesteal (e.g., Alucard, Yu Zhong, Uranus):
    • Replace Brute Force Breastplate/Guardian Helmet with: Dominance Ice (Reduces enemy HP regen and attack speed)
  • Against Heavy Magic Damage (e.g., Chang'e, Vale):
    • Replace Brute Force Breastplate/Hunter Strike with: Athena's Shield (Magic Damage absorption shield).
  • Against High Physical Defense (e.g., Tanky Fighters, Tanks):
    • Replace Brute Force Breastplate with: Malefic Roar (Significant Physical Penetration)
  • Against High Crowd Control (e.g., Nana, Aurora):
    • Consider building Tough Boots early and prioritize positioning.
    • Replace Brute Force Breastplate with: Rose Gold Meteor (Magic resistance and a shield when low HP) to provide an additional layer of protection against magic damage.

II. Roaming Terizla (Niche)

Role: Crowd Control Initiator, Damage Soaker, Objective Contester

Playstyle: Roaming Terizla focuses on providing crowd control and disruption for the team. He needs to rely on his allies to follow up on his engages. He is vulnerable to early game invades and must be very careful with his mana usage.

Build 1: Utility Roam

  • Item Build:
    1. Courage Mask: Core roaming item, provides movement speed and buffs to allies.
    2. Tough Boots: Reduces crowd control duration.
    3. Antique Cuirass: (Same as above)
    4. Oracle: (Same as above)
    5. Immortality: (Same as above)
    6. Guardian Helmet: (Same as above)
  • Emblem: Tank Emblem
    • Tier 1: Vitality (HP)
    • Tier 2: Firmness (Physical Defense)
    • Tier 3: Brave Smite (HP regen on crowd control)

Explanation: This build focuses on maximizing utility and tankiness for the team fights. Prioritize setting up kills for your teammates and protecting them from harm.

Build 2: Aggressive Roam

  • Item Build:
    1. Dire Hit: Early game damage amplification.
    2. / Warrior Boots/Tough Boots: (Same as above)
    3. Bloodlust Axe: (Same as above)
    4. Hunter Strike: (Same as above)
    5. Immortality: (Same as above)
    6. Brute Force Breastplate: (Same as above)
  • Emblem: Assassin Emblem
    • Tier 1: Agility (Movement Speed)
    • Tier 2: Invasion (Physical Penetration)
    • Tier 3: Killing Spree (HP and Movement Speed after kill)

Explanation: Provides early game damage and allows Terizla to snowball, however you will be quite squishy and reliant on getting early game gold to finish your items.

Counter Items (Roaming):

  • Against High Crowd Control Teams:
    • Tough Boots are essential. Consider building Rose Gold Meteor or Athena's Shield (if against heavy magic damage).
  • Against Burst Damage Assassins:
    • Antique Cuirass and Brute Force Breastplate (if physical) or Athena's Shield and Oracle (if magical).

General Ruby Tips & Tricks:

  • Skill Priority: Maximize your 1st Skill (Be Good!) first for wave clear and poke.
  • Lifesteal is Key: Ruby's primary source of sustain is her spell vamp. Prioritize items that provide spell vamp to maximize her survivability.
  • Crowd Control: Use your skills to interrupt enemy skills, lock down enemy heroes, and disrupt their team formations.
  • Positioning: Ruby is a frontline fighter, but she is also vulnerable to burst damage. Position yourself carefully in team fights to avoid getting focused down.
  • Master her passive: Ruby gains movement speed when she cast skill. Use it to chase enemy or to reposition for initiating team fights.
  • Ultimate Usage: Ruby's ultimate (I'm Offended!) is a powerful crowd control tool. Use it to lock down enemy carries, peel for your teammates, or initiate team fights.

Remember to adapt your build based on the enemy team composition and your team's needs. Mobile Legends is a dynamic game, and flexibility is key to success. Good luck dominating the Land of Dawn with Ruby!
