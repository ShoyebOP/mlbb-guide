Cici: The Agile Acrobat - A Comprehensive Guide

Alright, let's craft a comprehensive Mobile Legends: Bang Bang guide for Cici!

Primary Role: Fighter

Lane: EXP Lane

Cici's Strengths:

  • High Mobility
  • High Burst Damage
  • Good Sustain
  • Excellent at chasing enemies

Cici's Weaknesses:

  • Squishy
  • Susceptible to Crowd Control
  • Weak against Burst Damage
  • Item Dependent

Gameplay

Cici is a high-mobility fighter that excels at chasing down enemies and dealing burst damage. She is best played in the EXP lane where she can farm safely and scale into the late game.

Early Game:

Focus on farming and poking the enemy laner. Use your skills to clear waves and avoid getting ganked. Be mindful of your positioning and don't overextend.

Mid Game:

Continue to farm and look for opportunities to rotate to other lanes and help your team secure objectives. Be mindful of your mana usage and don't waste your skills.

Late Game:

Focus on dealing damage in team fights. Use your skills to poke the enemy team and burst down high-priority targets. Be mindful of your positioning and don't get caught out of position.
