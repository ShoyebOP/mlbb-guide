Sun: The Monkey King - A Comprehensive Guide

Alright, here's a comprehensive MLBB guide for Sun, focusing on his primary and secondary lane roles, builds, emblems, talents, and counter items.

Primary Role: Fighter

Lane: EXP Lane

Sun's Strengths:

  • Excellent Split Pusher
  • High Sustain
  • Good at Taking Objectives
  • Can Confuse Enemies with his Clones

Sun's Weaknesses:

  • Weak Against Burst Damage
  • Susceptible to Crowd Control
  • Clone Dependent
  • Item Dependent

Gameplay

Sun is a fighter that excels at split pushing and taking objectives. He is best played in the EXP lane where he can farm safely and scale into the late game.

Early Game:

Focus on farming and avoiding unnecessary fights. Use your skills to clear waves and poke the enemy laner. Be mindful of your positioning and don't overextend.

Mid Game:

Continue to farm and look for opportunities to rotate to other lanes and help your team secure objectives. Be mindful of your mana usage and don't waste your skills.

Late Game:

Focus on split pushing and taking objectives. Use your clones to distract the enemy team and create opportunities for your team to engage. Be mindful of your positioning and don't get caught out of position.
