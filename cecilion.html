Cecilion: The Embrace of Night - A Comprehensive Guide

Alright, let's craft a comprehensive Mobile Legends: Bang Bang guide for Cecilion!

Primary Role: Mage

Lane: Mid Lane

Cecilion's Strengths:

  • High Magic Damage
  • Long Range
  • Good Scaling
  • Excellent wave clear

Cecilion's Weaknesses:

  • Low Mobility
  • Susceptible to ganks
  • Weak early game
  • Item dependent

Gameplay

Cecilion is a scaling mage that relies on stacking mana to increase his damage. He is best played in the mid lane where he can farm safely and scale into the late game.

Early Game:

Focus on farming and stacking mana. Use your skills to clear waves and avoid getting ganked. Be mindful of your positioning and don't overextend.

Mid Game:

Continue to farm and stack mana. Look for opportunities to rotate to other lanes and help your team secure objectives. Be mindful of your mana usage and don't waste your skills.

Late Game:

Focus on dealing damage in team fights. Use your skills to poke the enemy team and burst down high-priority targets. Be mindful of your positioning and don't get caught out of position.
